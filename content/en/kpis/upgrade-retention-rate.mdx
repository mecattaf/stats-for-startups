# Upgrade Retention Rate (URR)

Use this formula if you have a tiered product or service in which users can upgrade to a higher level. It helps to determine how pleased users are and what prompts an upsell. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Upgrade Retention Rate} = \frac{\textrm{\# of users upgraded during a timeframe}}{\textrm{Total \# of users}} [^1]
$$
