# Cost per Mille (CPM)

Cost per Mille or CPM is what you pay for 1,000 impressions. This was a popular way of determining ad costs for print ads, and it is still sometimes the best choice for digital advertising. [^1] [^2]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{CPM} = 1000\times \frac{\textrm{cost}}{\textrm{impressions}} [^1]
$$
