# Net Merchandise Value (NMV)

Net Margin Value is obtained by subtracting all the fees and expenses from the Gross Merchandise Value.  More realistic look into how business is actually performing as it takes into account costs, refunds, etc. [^1]  [^2]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{NMV = GMV - All costs (marketing and refunds and gateway payments)} [^1]
$$
