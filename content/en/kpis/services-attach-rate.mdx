# Services Attach Rate (SAR)

The attach rate, also commonly referred to as an attach ratio, is the number of add-on products/units sold in relation to a primary product/unit. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Attach Rate} = \frac{\textrm{\# of secondary products sold}}{\textrm{\# of primary products sold}} \times 100 [^1]
$$

- Attach Rate
