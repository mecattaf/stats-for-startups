# Accounts Receivable Turnover (ART)

The accounts receivable turnover KPI reflects the rate at which your business is successfully collecting payments due from your customers. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Accounts Receivable Turnover} = \frac{\textrm{Total sales for a period}}{\textrm{Average accounts receivable for that period}} [^1] ,\\
\textrm{Accounts Receivable Turnover} = \frac{\textrm{Net value of credit sales}}{\textrm{Average accounts receivable}} [^2]
$$
