# Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

Total volume of a particular product (or service) that can be sold by all vendors on the market within a territory that you are able to service for the planned period (usually a year, a quarter or a month). [^1] [^4] [^5] [^6] [^7] [^2] [^3]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{SAM} = \textrm{(Target Segment of TAM)} \times \textrm{ACV} [^1]
$$
