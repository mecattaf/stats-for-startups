# GRIT Score (GRIT)

Score Bessemer uses to define the attractiveness of a business investment [^1]
Some benchmarks: 3-4 is good, 4-6 is better, >6 is best

## Calculation

$$
GRIT score = \frac{\textrm{(ARR \%YoY Growth (Usually 50-200\%))}+\textrm{\% annual Net Retention} + \textrm{Years of Runway + Net New ARR}}{\textrm{Net Burn}} [^1]
$$
