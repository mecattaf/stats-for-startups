# Average Transaction Value (ATV)

The ATV of your business is the average dollar amount that a consumer spends with your business in a single transaction. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Average Transaction Value} = \frac{\textrm{Total value of all transactions}}{\textrm{\# of transactions or sales}}  [^1]
$$
