# Total Addressable Market (TAM)

Total addressable market or TAM refers to the total market demand for a product or service. It’s the maximum amount of revenue a business can possibly generate by selling their product or service in a specific market. [^1] [^4] [^5] [^6] [^7] [^2] [^3]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{TAM} = \textrm{Average Revenue Per User} \times \textrm{Number of Customers in the Target Market} [^1],\\
\newline
\textrm{TAM} = \textrm{Annual Contract Value} \times \textrm{(\# of Possible Accounts)} [^2],\\
\newline
\textrm{TAM} = \textrm{[ACV $\times$ (Small and medium sized business (SMB) \#)]} \\
+ \textrm{[ACV $\times$  Mid Market Accounts (MM) \#]}  \\
+ \textrm{[ACV $\times$ large enterprises (EE) \#]} [^3]
$$
