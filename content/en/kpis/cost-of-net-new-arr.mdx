# Cost of Net New ARR (CNA)

The cost to acquire one net new dollar of ARR. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Cost of New New ARR} = \frac{\textrm{Sales \& Marketing Spend}}{\textrm{Net New ARR}} [^1]
$$
