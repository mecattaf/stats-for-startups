# Gross Burn Rate (GBR)

How much money you keep from every dollar spent on making and selling the product. This evaluates a company's operating expenses.It is calculated by summing all its operating expenses such as rent, salaries, and other overhead, and is often measured on a monthly basis. It also provides insight into a companyâ€™s cost drivers and efficiency, regardless of revenue. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Gross Burn Rate} = \frac{Cash}{\textrm{Monthly operating Expenses}} [^1],

\textrm{Gross Burn Rate} = \frac{Expenses}{\textrm{Time Periods During Measurement}} [^2]
$$
