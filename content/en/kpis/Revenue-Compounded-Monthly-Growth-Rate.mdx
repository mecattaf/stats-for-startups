# Revenue Compounded Monthly Growth Rate (RCM)

Implied compounded monthly revenue growth rate between two disparate months [^1] [^2]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Revenue CMGR} = {\frac{\textrm{latest month revenu}}{\textrm{first month revenue}}}^{\frac{1}{\textrm{\# months}}}-1 [^1]
$$
