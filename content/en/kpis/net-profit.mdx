# Net Profit (NP)

Net profit, sometimes referred to as the bottom line or net income, is the amount left over after all expenses, including the cost of goods sold, operating expenses, and debt, have been accounted for. [^1] [^2]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Net Profit} = \textrm{Revenue - COGS - Operating and other expenses - interest - taxes} [^1],

\textrm{Net earnings} = \textrm{Total Revenue - Total Expenses (including operating expenses and financing costs and taxes)} [^2]
$$
