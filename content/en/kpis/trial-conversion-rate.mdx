# Trial Conversion Rate (TCR)

The number of free customers that become paid customers after a defined time period. [^1]
Especially relevant for the Freemium revenue model.

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Trial Conversion Rate} = \frac{\textrm{Total number of free to paid customer conversions in a given period}}{\textrm{Total number of free users in the same period}} [^1]
$$
