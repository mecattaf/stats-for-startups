# Gross retention (GRtn)

Revenue retained, divided by total revenue form the previous time period [^1] [^2]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Gross retention} = \frac{\textrm{retained revenue}}{\textrm{revenue from last period}} [^1]
$$
