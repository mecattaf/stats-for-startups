# Gross MRR Churn (GMC)

Monthly recurring revenue lost in a given month, compared to that at the beginning month [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Gross MRR Churn} = \frac{\textrm{Monthly revenue lost in a given month}}{\textrm{Monthly recurring revenue at the beginning of the month}} [^1]
$$
