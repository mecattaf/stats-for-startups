# Percentage Active Users (PAU)

Total monthly active users with a registered account ("logged in") divided by the total unique visitors (inclusive of both "logged-in" and "logged-out") during the same 28-day window [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Percent Active Users} = \frac{\textrm{Total active with a registered account}}{\textrm{Total unique visitors}} \times 100 [^1]
$$
