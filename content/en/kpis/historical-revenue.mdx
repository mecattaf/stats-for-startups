# Historical Revenue (HRev)

Revenue is the income generated from normal business operations and includes discounts and deductions for returned merchandise. Historical revenue is the revenue that has been actually realized by the company. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Historical Revenue} = \textrm{Sales Price}  \times \textrm{Number of Units Sold} [^1],

\textrm{Historical Revenue} = GMV \times \textrm{Take Rate} [^2],

\textrm{Historical Revenue} = \textrm{Number of customers} \times \textrm{Average price of services provided} [^3]
$$
