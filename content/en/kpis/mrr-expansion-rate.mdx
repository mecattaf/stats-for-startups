# MRR expansion rate (MExp)

MRR expansion rate is the additional MRR generated for your business that comes from existing customers, usually in the form of add-ons or up-sells. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{MRR expansion rate} = \frac{\textrm{expansion MRR at end of month} - \textrm{expansion MRR at beginning of month}}{\textrm{Expansion MRR at beginning of month}} [^1]
$$
