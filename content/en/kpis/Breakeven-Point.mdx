# Breakeven Point (BEP)

The time it takes an organization to break even from its investment in a new product or process. If the costs are big up front, this measure can help you understand how long it will take to recoup these expenses. [^1] [^2]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Breakeven} = \frac{\textrm{fixed expenses}} {1 â€“ \frac{\textrm{variable expenses}}{\textrm{sales}}} [^1] , 
\textrm{Breakeven} = \frac{\textrm{fixed costs}}{\textrm{unit selling price} - \textrm{variable costs}} [^2]
$$
