# Fundraising Ask (Ask)

Amount of funding (USD) the company states it is seeking for an upcoming funding round. [^1] [^2] [^3] [^4]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Ask} = \textrm{Post-Money Valuation} - \textrm{Pre-Money Valuation}
$$
