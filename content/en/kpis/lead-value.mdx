# Lead Value (LV)

The economic value of a new lead. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Lead Value} = \frac{\textrm{Value of sale}}{\textrm{Number of leads}} [^1],

\textrm{Lead Value} = \textrm{Average Sale} \times \textrm{Conversion Rate} [^2]
$$
