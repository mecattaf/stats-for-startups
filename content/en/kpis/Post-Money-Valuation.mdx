# Post-Money Valuation (Post)

The post-money valuation is the total of the pre-money valuation plus the additional equity injected into the company. [^1] [^2] [^3] [^4] [^5] [^6]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Post-Money Valuation}= \textrm{Pre-Money Valuation} + \textrm{Investment Amount} [^1] , 
\textrm{Post Money Valuation} ={\textrm{ Investment}}{\textrm{\% of equity}} [^2]
$$
