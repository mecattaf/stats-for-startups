# Customer Lifetime (Lftm)

Customer lifetime is the multiplicative inverse of the customer churn rate. If customer churn rate is a monthly % or yearly %, then the Customer Lifetime will be for the same time period. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Customer Lifetime} = \frac{1}{\textrm{Customer Churn Rate}}
$$
