# Net ARR (nARR)

Net yearly recurring revenue refers to the yearly value of newly acquired accounts to your sales system and yearly added value to current accounts, minus the value lost from closed or reduced accounts. [^1]

Net ARR is increasing on an absolute basis quarter over quarter). A flat Net ARR quarter over quarter (QoQ) implies a company is growing linearly; a growing Net ARR QoQ implies a company is growing exponentially.  [^2]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Net ARR} = \textrm{New ARR + Upsell ARR - Downsell ARR - Churn ARR} [^1]
$$
