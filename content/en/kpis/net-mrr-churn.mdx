# Net MRR Churn (NMC)

The number that will go negative if the Expansion revenue from existing customers starts to outstrip the lost revenue from churn. Getting to negative Net MRR Churn is a great goal for a SaaS company. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{\% Net MRR churn} = \frac{\textrm{(Churned MRR - Expansion MRR)}}{\textrm{Previous Months MRR}}
$$
