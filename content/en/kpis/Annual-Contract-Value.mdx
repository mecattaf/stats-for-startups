# Annual Contract Value (ACV)

Annual Contract Value (or ACV) is the annual yearly revenue generated from each customer contract. [^1] [^2] [^3]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{ACV} =\frac{\textrm{Total contract value (excluding one-time fees)}}{\textrm{Total Years In Contract}} [^1]
$$
