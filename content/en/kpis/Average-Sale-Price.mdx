# Average Sale Price (ASP)

Average Selling Price (ASP) is the average price a given product is sold for. [^1] [^2] [^3] [^4]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{ASP} = \frac{\textrm{Total New MRR during a period}}{\textrm{\# of customers gained during period}} [^1], 
\textrm{ASP} = \frac{\textrm{Sum(Product or Service Revenue)}} {\textrm{Count(Products or Services sold)}} [^2]
$$
