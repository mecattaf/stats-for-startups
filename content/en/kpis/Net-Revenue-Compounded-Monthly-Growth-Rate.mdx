# Net Revenue Compounded Monthly Growth Rate (nRCM)

Implied compounded monthly net revenue growth rate between two disparate months [^1] [^2]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Net Revenue CMGR} = (\frac{\textrm{latest month net revenue}}{\textrm{first month net revenue}})^{\frac{1}{\#\textrm{ months}}}-1 [^1]
$$
