# Net Churn (NCrn)

The sum of losses minus gains in revenue from existing customers only (exclding new customers), divided by total revenue from the previous time period. Net churn is the sum of losses minus gains in revenue from existing customers only (i.e., excluding new customers) divided by total revenue from the previous time period. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Net Churn} = \frac{\textrm{churned} + \textrm{contraction} - \textrm{resurrected} - \textrm{expansions}}{\textrm{revenue from previous period}} [^1],  

\textrm{Net Churn} = \frac{\textrm{MRR lost} - \textrm{MRR from upselss} (\textrm{this month})}{\textrm{MRR at the beginning of the month}} [^2]
$$
