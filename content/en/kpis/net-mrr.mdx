# Net MRR (nMRR)

Net monthly recurring revenue refers to the monthly value of newly acquired accounts to your sales system and monthly added value to current accounts, minus the value lost from closed or reduced accounts. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Net MRR} = \textrm{New MRR + Expansion MRR + Reduction MRR - Loss MRR} [^1]
$$
