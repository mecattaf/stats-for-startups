# Net New ARR (nNARR)

Net new ARR equals ARR from new business acquisition and the incremental ARR from expansion and upsell.  [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Net New ARR} = \textrm{New ARR from new customers + expansion ARR form existing customers - Churned ARR} [^1],

\textrm{Net New ARR} = \textrm{Current Year ARR - Previous Year ARR} [^2]
$$
