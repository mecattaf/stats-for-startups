# Purchase Frequency (PF)

Purchase Frequency is a metric that shows the average number of times a customer makes a purchase within a set time frame, usually within a year. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Purchase Frequency} = \frac{\textrm{\# of orders}}{\textrm{\# of unique customers}} [^1]
$$
