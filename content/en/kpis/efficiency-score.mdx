# Efficiency Score (ES)

Efficiency score is calculated by dividing the net new ARR by the net burn [^1]
Good is <0.5x, Better is 0.5x-1.5x, Best is >1.5x [^2]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Efficiency Score} = \frac{\textrm{Net New ARR}}{\textrm{Net Burn}} [^1]
$$
