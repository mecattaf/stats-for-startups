# Working Capital (WCap)

Cash that is immediately available is "working capital". Cash on hand, accounts receivable, short-term investments are all included, as well as accounts payable, accrued expenses, and loans are all part of this KPI equation. [^1] [^2] [^3] [^4] [^5] [^6]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Working Capital} = \textrm{Existing assets} - \textrm{existing liabilities} [^1]
$$
