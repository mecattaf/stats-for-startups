# Market Share (MktS)

The percentage of the addressable market your product has penetrated. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Market share of target segment} = \frac{\textrm{Value of current market segment}}{\textrm{Value of target market (usually SOM)}}  [^1]
$$
