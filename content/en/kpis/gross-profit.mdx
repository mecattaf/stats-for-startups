# Gross Profit (GP)

The income a business has left, after paying all direct expenses related to the manufacturing of a product. The calculation of gross profit only takes into account the variable expenses involved with generating revenue. [^1]

## Calculation

$$
\textrm{Gross Profit = Revenue - COGS}  [^1]
$$
